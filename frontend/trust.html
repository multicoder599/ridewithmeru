Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue? +
You can report any issues directly through the app or by visiting our office in Meru Town. Our support team is available 24/7.
Is my payment info safe? +
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and direct M-Pesa STK Push technology. We never store your PIN.
How are delivery prices calculated? +
Prices are based on a transparent per-kilometer rate set by the intermediary to ensure fairness for both the customer and the rider.
